Healthcare Patient Analytics & Cost Dashboard📌 Project ObjectiveThe objective of this project was to analyze a dataset of 500 patient records to uncover key insights regarding patient demographics, health conditions, and treatment costs.📊 Key Insights & FindingsAge and Hospital Visits: The 60+ age group has the highest average hospital visits, showing a sharp upward trend as patients enter the senior demographic.Treatment Costs: The overall average treatment cost across the facility is $10,724.50. Non-diabetic patients have a slightly higher average treatment cost ($11,021.85) compared to diabetic patients ($10,401.32).BMI Correlation: There is no linear correlation between a patient's BMI and their treatment cost, with a statistical correlation coefficient near zero (-0.001). The average patient BMI is 27.62.Demographics: The patient population is relatively balanced, consisting of 258 Male patients (51.6%) and 242 Female patients (48.4%).Lifestyle Factors: Smoking status did not show a drastic impact on treatment costs or visits. Non-smokers averaged 7.28 visits and $10,897 in costs, while smokers averaged 7.24 visits and $10,585 in costs.📈 Dashboard FeaturesThe interactive Healthcare Patient Analytics & Cost Dashboard  allows users to:Filter data dynamically by selecting Gender, Diabetes Status, and Smoking Status.View high-level metrics including total hospital visits (3,631).Analyze visual data representations such as Age Distribution charts and BMI vs. Treatment Cost scatter plots.🛠️ Tools UsedData Analytics & VisualizationPower BI Powerquery
